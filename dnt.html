Tourist Attractions in Dantewada

Danteshwari Temple

This ancient temple is dedicated to Goddess Danteshwari and is an important pilgrimage site. Located at the confluence of the holy rivers Shankini and Dhankini, the temple holds great religious significance and attracts devotees from all over the country, especially during the Dussehra festival.

Dholkal

Dholkal is known for its scenic beauty and historical significance. The site is famous for the ancient Ganesha idol perched atop a hill, which offers a panoramic view of the surrounding forests. The trek to Dholkal is adventurous and popular among nature enthusiasts and trekkers.

Sarthdhar

Sarthdhar is a beautiful waterfall located in the Dantewada district. It is an ideal picnic spot and offers a refreshing escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. The falls are surrounded by lush greenery, making it a perfect spot for nature lovers and photographers.

Bacheli

Bacheli is famous for its lush green landscapes and local culture. It is a small town known for its scenic beauty and is an excellent place to experience the rich tribal culture of the region. The town also serves as a base for exploring the nearby attractions.

Barsur Temple

Barsur is known for its ancient temples and historical significance. Once a bustling town with 147 temples and numerous ponds, Barsur is now a site of archaeological interest. The Mama-Bhanja temple and the Battisa temple are notable for their architectural beauty and historical importance.

Fulpad Waterfalls

Fulpad Waterfalls is a popular spot for its natural beauty and serene environment. The cascading water, surrounded by dense forest, makes it a perfect spot for a day trip. Visitors can enjoy a refreshing dip in the cool waters and have a relaxing time amidst nature.
